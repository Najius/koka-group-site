Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our corporate services

What types of corporate events do you cover?

Conferences, galas, student parties, trade shows, product launches, team buildings and after parties. Each service is tailored to your brand and objectives.

Can you handle the sound for an entire conference?

Absolutely. Speeches, panel discussions, award ceremonies, evening party: we adapt our configuration to each part of the programme for flawless results.

Do you offer solutions for product launches?

Yes. A striking sonic and visual atmosphere to capture attention and showcase your product. Lighting design, bespoke sonic identity and polished staging.

What technical equipment do you use?

Sound systems adapted to each venue, modular stage lighting, Pioneer decks and high-end controllers. Backup equipment is always on hand.

Do you work throughout France?

Yes, all across France from our bases in Nantes and Poitiers. We always carry out a technical survey beforehand to tailor our setup to each space.

What is the booking process?

Contact us via the form or WhatsApp. We discuss your needs, then send you a personalised quote. Full coordination with your event team.
