Artistic collaborations

Frequently asked questions

Everything about our artistic services

Do you compose original tracks for fashion shows?

Yes, we compose original tracks on Ableton, tailor-made for your collections, in close collaboration with the designer to reflect the brand's identity.

Can you collaborate with artists on installations?

Absolutely. We collaborate with visual artists, videographers and cultural institutions to design immersive sound installations, seamlessly integrated into the exhibition space.

What music production tools do you use?

Ableton Live for composition, synthesisers and MIDI controllers in the studio. Pioneer turntables and tailored sound systems for live performances.

Do you perform as opening acts for concerts?

Yes, at venues and festivals alike. We adapt our set to the headlining artist's world with exclusive mashups when needed.

Do you work in museums and galleries?

Yes: museums, galleries, cultural centres, heritage sites and alternative venues. We adapt our equipment to the acoustic and aesthetic requirements of each space.
