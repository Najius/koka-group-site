Our approach

Every moment deserves its own music

Your event deserves a production that brings out its full intensity. KoKa Group orchestrates every detail: a finely tuned soundscape, lighting that sculpts the atmosphere, subtle effects that transform the moment into a timeless experience. We design moments that reflect who you are, where every note and every light tells a story — yours.

Ceremony & cocktail hour

Elegant background music for the ceremony and cocktail reception. We create a refined, understated atmosphere that accompanies each moment with just the right touch.

Evening party

From the first slow dance to the last song, we build the energy progressively to keep the dancefloor packed all night long. Playlist approved by you, adapted in real time.

Hen & stag parties

One last celebration before the big day? We put together a tailor-made party with music that matches your vibe and lighting effects that set the tone.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our wedding services

What music styles do you offer for weddings?

No limits on style. Pop, house, funk, disco, hip-hop, Latin music and more — we tailor our selection to your tastes during the planning meeting.

Can you handle both the cocktail hour AND the evening party?

Absolutely. Our packages cover the entire day: ceremony, cocktail hour, dinner and evening party. In fact, we recommend this approach to ensure perfect harmony from start to finish.

What equipment do you use?

Premium professional gear: sound systems adapted to your venue, latest-generation decks, LED lighting, moving heads, fog machines. Everything is maintained and calibrated for flawless results.

Which regions do you cover?

Based in Nantes and Poitiers, we work throughout France and internationally on request. Travel costs are transparently included in our quotes.

How do I book your services?

Get in touch via the form, by phone or WhatsApp. Free discovery meeting, then a personalised quote. A deposit confirms the booking. We recommend reaching out 6 to 12 months in advance.

Do you offer custom lighting?

Yes, lighting is an integral part of our service: uplights in your colour scheme, dancefloor lighting, custom gobos with your initials. Light is a key element of our approach.
