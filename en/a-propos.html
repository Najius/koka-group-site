About us

KoKa Group is an events and creative arts agency founded in 2022 by Camille and Colin, two music enthusiasts and staunch advocates of a job well done.

The agency stands out through its artistic approach to the events industry, combining technical precision and creativity to deliver memorable experiences where every detail matters.

We speak French, English, Spanish and Italian

Camille

Curious Inventive Connector Determined

Born in 1991, Camille has always been fascinated by sound in all its forms. From well-worn cassette tapes to early explorations of electronic music, he quickly developed eclectic tastes. Big Beat, Jungle, Indie Rock, Jazz... his influences multiplied with every new encounter.

As a teenager, he joined his first bands and took up instrumental practice. Later, his solo projects blended acoustic and electronic production. In 2014, he made his debut behind the decks at clubs such as Le Loft, SC Club and Pink Paradise.

After moving to Rennes in 2010, he immersed himself in the world of live performance: festivals (Transmusicales, Le Mans Fait Son Cirque), venues (Le 4Bis, Jardin Moderne). As founder of the Book'n Co association, he championed emerging local artists.

In 2022, he co-founded KoKa Group with Colin. Today, he continues to explore new horizons: composition, vocals, voice-over and artistic DJ sets.

Colin

Passionate Explorer Entrepreneur Creative

Colin has been a DJ and music lover for as long as he can remember. Before stepping behind the decks, he honed his ear as a drummer. His influences are wide-ranging: from Daft Punk to Birdy Nam Nam, through Massive Attack, Étienne de Crécy and the French Touch 2.0 scene.

In 2014, he enrolled at UCPA DJ School in Poitiers, graduating top of his class in 2016. Event DJ in Mayenne, resident in Angers, he performed at the warm-up of the inaugural La Nuit Electro edition.

In 2018, Berlin was a turning point: he explored new sounds there and wove that raw, experimental energy into his style. Back in France in 2022, he became a regular DJ at BB25 (Bordeaux) then at La Locomotive (Poitiers).

His collaboration with Carbon Killer on the Synthwave/Darkwave project earned over 1.3 million Spotify streams with "The Last Stand", and saw him on iconic stages: l'Iboat, le Blacklab, and La Cigale for Shaârghot's 10th anniversary in 2025.
