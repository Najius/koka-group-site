Frequently asked questions

Everything about our private event services

What types of private events do you cover?

Birthdays, garden parties, pool parties, themed celebrations, New Year's Eve, parties with friends. From 20 to 200 guests, we tailor our service to create the perfect atmosphere.

Can you adapt to a specific theme?

Absolutely. Disco night, tropical evening, 80s theme or an original concept: we design the soundtrack and lighting to match your vision.

What minimum space is required?

We adapt to all spaces. A minimum of 15 m2 for the technical setup, plus a dance area proportional to the number of guests. We assess everything during the site visit.

Do you offer stag/hen party packages?

Yes. Villa, private bar or outdoors: we create a festive, fully tailored atmosphere with music that matches your vibe.

How far in advance should I book?

2 to 3 months minimum, 4 to 6 months during peak season (June-September). Don't hesitate to contact us even for last-minute requests.
